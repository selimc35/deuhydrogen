Projects

TÜBİTAK 1001 (2025 - 2027) – Range Extension through Effective Thermal Management

This project focuses on enhance the energy efficiency and extend the driving range of urban light-duty electric vehicles by optimizing the integrated thermal management of key components such as the fuel cell, battery, and metal hydride (MH) based hydrogen tank.

Dokuz Eylül University BAP (2023 - 2025) – Investigation of Thermal Management Systems for Batteries and Metal Hydride Tanks

This project focuses on thermal behavior and cooling/heating strategies for battery-MH hybrid systems.
