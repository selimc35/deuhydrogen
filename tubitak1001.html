TÜBİTAK 1001 Research Project

Our research group is conducting a TÜBİTAK 1001-funded project focuses on enhance the energy efficiency and extend the driving range of urban light-duty electric vehicles by optimizing the integrated thermal management of key components such as the fuel cell, battery, and metal hydride (MH) based hydrogen tank.
